Common Questions

What is Retro Radio?
Retro Radio is your classic sound companion, bringing the nostalgia of vintage radio to the modern era.
How do I listen to Retro Radio?
You can listen to Retro Radio through our website by selecting your favorite stations or tuning in to themed playlists.
Is there a mobile app available?
Yes, we offer a mobile app for both Android and iOS devices, available for download on their respective app stores.
Can I request specific songs?
Currently, we offer a curated selection of timeless tunes. We're working on expanding our song library in the future.
How can I contact customer support?
You can reach our support team by visiting our Support page and following the provided instructions.
Is there a subscription fee?
Yes, we offer both free and premium subscription plans. Our premium plan offers an ad-free experience and additional features.
Can I create my own playlists?
Currently, we provide pre-made themed playlists, but we're planning to introduce a playlist creation feature in the near future.
Do you offer offline listening?
Offline listening is a feature available to our premium subscribers, allowing you to enjoy your favorite tunes without an internet connection.
